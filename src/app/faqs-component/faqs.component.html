General questions

What is advertXpo?

advertXpo is a technology platform that helps advertisers connect with their audiences through targeted and effective advertising on screens found in public locations.

How do I change my Phone number?

You may not necessarily have to change the phone number. If you want to use a different phone number to make a payment, you just go to the create advert page and scroll down to the payment method. Once you select the MTN mobile money option, you will be given the option to enter the phone number that you want to use for payment.
Advertisers

How do I know that my advert has been displayed?

You will receive an SMS notification and/or email notification once the advert is being displayed. You will also receive a notification once your campaign period expires. But most importantly, conversion of customers is the most fool-proof for a return on your investment.

How do I attach an image to the advert?

On the create advert page , Select the 3- Screen Split option. Once this is selected, the option for images will be highlighted in Orange with a white text. Click the black button option that has “Upload images” text written in white. Note, that you can upload a maximum of 3 images and they must be of supported formats i.e. jpg, png, gif. The selected image will be displayed with a heading of “Advert Images” above the “Upload images” button. Once the image or images are selected the selected image will be relayed onto the “AD Preview” on the top right corner of the page. You can be able to change the images by clicking on the cancel symbol presented on top of the uploaded image.
